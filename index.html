Our Mission

Helping you stay focused on what really matters

People often have a difficulty completing tasks because of distractions, poor time management, and/or an unhealthy work-life balance.

Petivity aims to increase productivity by effectively eliminating distractions and keeping people on track in a fun and engaging way.

Low Fidelity Prototype

Fail fast. Fail often.

Because design changes are less expensive earlier in the development process, it's important to hammer out the bigger details on paper. For this reason, we built a low fidelity prototype and tested our product with several users.

For more information on our low-fi prototype download the slides in powerpoint or pdf format, or read our report.

Medium-Fi Prototype

Testing our User Interface with an Interactive Prototyping Tool

After receiving valuable feedback on our low-fi prototype, we iterated on our design and improved the functionality and usability of our product's key features. We then built and tested a higher quality, medium-fi prototype using Marvel's interactive user interface design tool.

For more information on our medium-fi prototype, download our slides in powerpoint or pdf format, or take a look at our ReadMe file.
